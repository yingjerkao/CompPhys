Resources for instructors and students

This web site contains resources that accompany the book Computational Physics by Mark Newman, including sample chapters from the book, programs and data used in the examples and exercises, the text of all the exercises themselves, and copies of all figures from the book. You are welcome to download any of the material on these pages and print it out, use it for instruction or learning, or just read it for your own interest. Feedback is welcome. Contact details are on the right.
